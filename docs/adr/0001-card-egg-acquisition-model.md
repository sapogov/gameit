# Card and Egg Acquisition Model

Status: accepted

GameIt Monsters uses Cards as the primary acquisition surface for Creatures, Farms, Materials, and Buffs. Creature Cards preserve rolled stats and two pre-existing Attacks; common Creature Cards convert directly into Creatures with Magic Dust, while uncommon and rarer Creature Cards become Card-made Eggs that preserve the visible inherited stats and Attacks before hatching. This keeps Packs central to progression, makes individual Cards meaningful, and avoids wild catching as the default acquisition loop.
